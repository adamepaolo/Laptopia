ThinkPad T14s Gen 5 Intel (14”) - Eclipse Black

System Specs:

Processor: Intel® Core™ Ultra 5 125U Processor (E-cores up to 3.60 GHz P-cores up to 4.30 GHz)

Operating System: Windows 11 Home Lenovo recommends Windows 11 Pro for business.

Graphic Card: Integrated Intel® Graphics

Memory: 16 GB LPDDR5X-6400MHz (Soldered))

Storage: 256 GB SSD M.2 2280 PCIe Gen4 TLC Opal

Features

Adaptive Power, Better Endurance

The ThinkPad T14s Gen 5 laptop prudently adapts to your workload needs with an improved battery system. Its advanced thermal design, featuring dual fans and well-placed rear air vents, ensures optimal cooling efficiency. And, its MIL-STD-810H tested for high-end durability and engineered to be power-efficient with an ENERGY STAR® 8.0 certification.

Peace of Mind with Smarter Security

The ThinkPad T14s Gen 5 laptop boasts robust security features, including optional biometric authentication with fingerprint reader and facial recognition for quick, secure login. ThinkShield, our comprehensive security suite, ensures data encryption via a Discrete Trusted Platform Module (dTPM). And for added protection, there’s a webcam privacy shutter.

Responsibly Made for a Better Tomorrow

While the slim profile of T14s laptop is sure to impress, take pride in knowing its keyboard frame utilizes 90% recycled magnesium and the bottom cover features 55% recycled aluminum. Both colour options include speaker, battery, and adapter enclosures made of 90% post-consumer content (PCC) recycled plastic and come in plastic-free packaging.
