IdeaPad 5i 2-in-1 (16″ Intel) - Luna Grey

System Specs:

Processor: Intel® Core™ 5 120U Processor (E-cores up to 3.80 GHz P-cores up to 5.00 GHz)

Operating System: Windows 11 Home 64

Graphic Card: Integrated Intel® Graphics

Memory: 8 GB LPDDR5-5200MHz (Soldered)

Storage: 256 512 GB SSD M.2 2242 PCIe Gen4 TLC

Features

All Systems Ahead

Pausing is not an option on the IdeaPad 5i 2-in-1 laptop, with up to Intel® Core™ 7 processors, unleashing exhilarating performance to throttle through your deadlines and boss battles. Whether you’re building your portfolio, mastering a new hobby, or expressing your creativity, you have the speed and power to accomplish them effortlessly. It features plenty of memory, so all your apps and programs run like clockwork. Plus, it comes with extensive storage — every group project and memorable moment can be stored safely with you.

Because One Mode Doesn't Fit All

There are endless possibilities with the versatile IdeaPad 5i 2-in-1 laptop, which folds back on a 360° hinge for amazing flexibility. Knock out a business plan on laptop mode, stream the latest episode on tent, go into stand mode for presentations, or sketch your next idea on a tablet. Made for easy carrying, it is crafted with a sophisticated metal cover and is finished in either Luna Grey or Cosmic Blue.

Dial Up Your Entertainment

Every moment on the IdeaPad 5i 2-in-1’s larger display is a cinematic one, with stunning rich colours. Thin bezels mean more screen real estate for a fuller viewing experience. And it delivers remarkable sound to match, thanks to Dolby Audio™-certified speakers that pump acoustics of remarkable depth, detail, and volume. Work for extended periods without straining your eyes, thanks to TÜV Low Blue Light certification.
